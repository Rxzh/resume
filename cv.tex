\section{Summary}
PhD candidate (graduating 2026) at Mines Paris -- PSL University and CNRS, specializing in \textbf{self-supervised and multimodal machine learning} for scientific data. My research focuses on learning structured latent representations of microstructure and mechanical testing data (EBSD, nanoindentation, acoustic emission) to detect anomalies and infer crystal elastoplasticity properties at the sub-grain level. I run an AI-based OSINT SaaS company (VH Systems) serving Fortune 500 clients, and I am independently exploring long-horizon video prediction with V-JEPA. All research code and datasets are open-sourced.
